About AI Career Path

AI Career Path is a tool designed to help you assess your skills in various AI-related fields and find a personalized career path. Our assessments are powered by the latest in generative AI to provide you with relevant and challenging questions. Our goal is to empower you to learn and grow in the exciting world of artificial intelligence.

Whether you're a student, a professional looking to switch careers, or an expert wanting to validate your skills, AI Career Path is here to guide you.
